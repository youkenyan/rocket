Our Sponsors

Gold Sponsors

ANSYS is a global leader in engineering simulation software, providing tools that enable engineers to predict how product designs will behave in real-world environments. Their software is essential for ensuring reliability and performance, helping engineers to solve complex design challenges. As a GOLD sponsor, ANSYS provides vital support to Algonquin College Rocketry by equipping us with the simulation tools needed to optimize and test our rocket designs.

Space Simulation Services of Canada Corporation (SSSOC) specializes in providing space environment simulation services. Their expertise includes creating accurate space-like conditions for testing spacecraft and related technologies. As a GOLD sponsor, SSSOC offers both financial support and technical insights, playing a critical role in our ability to simulate and test our rocketry projects under realistic conditions.

Silver Sponsors

Canadian Defense Review (CDR) is Canada’s leading defence and military journal, serving the information needs of Canada’s defence community. CDR's readership includes senior officers in the Canadian military, senior government officials, and executives in the defence, aerospace, security, and related industries. The magazine provides comprehensive coverage of defence issues, making it an invaluable resource for stakeholders in Canada’s defence sector. Their support as a SILVER sponsor helps us stay informed and connected within the industry, enhancing our projects and initiatives.

Altium is a renowned provider of electronic design automation software, specializing in PCB design and embedded systems. Their advanced tools facilitate the creation of sophisticated electronic systems, crucial for modern rockets. Altium's SILVER sponsorship is instrumental in equipping our team with the necessary software to develop and refine the electronic components of our rocketry projects, ensuring high performance and reliability.

Bronze Sponsors

NordSpace is an emerging company in the space technology sector, dedicated to developing innovative solutions for space exploration. As a BRONZE sponsor, NordSpace provides foundational support that is essential for the growth and success of our rocketry projects. Their commitment to advancing space technology aligns with our goals of exploring new frontiers in rocketry.
