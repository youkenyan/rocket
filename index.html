About Us

ACR is a multidisciplinary team that aims to design and build a rocket that will reach Mach 1.7 and 17,000 feet. Our team is constantly improving our skills in various fields such as mechanical, electrical, robotics, and computer engineering, as well as business, marketing, and graphic design.

Our Mission

ACR’s mission is to create the best rockets possible, showing our skills and innovation while improving our abilities and representing Algonquin College.

We’re competing in the Basic Category of the 2024 Launch Canada Competition against Universities across Canada.

The Basic Category limits design to exclusively commercial off-the-shelf parts (COTS). In the future, we plan to participate in Launch Canada’s Advanced Category and the Spaceport American Cup.

Short Term

Design, test, build, and successfully launch our Rocket.

Reach a launch apogee of over 22,000ft and Mach 2.

Mid Term

Advance to higher levels of the Launch Canada competition.

Expand our outreach efforts.

Enhance our team self-sufficiency.

Long Term

Compete in future Launch Canada Competitions as well as Spaceport America Cup.

OutReach

ACR is committed to inspiring the next generation to pursue a fulfilling career in STEM. Our outreach program consists of donating 3D Printers and introducing 3D modeling to young students. This mission is directed at elementary and high schools, especially in low-income areas, with the goal to ignite a passion for engineering and STEM in young minds. Your brand will be associated with these noble initiatives, showcasing your commitment to social responsibility.
